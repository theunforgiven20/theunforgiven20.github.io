Development Process

Working in a two-man group, with both do not have much experience in the IT industry, we have faced many kinds of issues when it came to critical thinking and our group process was much slower than what we expected. In contrast, the fact that our group has fewer members than the other groups make this project less stressful, we can talk about the project at any time without having to have scheduled team meetings.

Challenges
May 2, 2022 - May 8, 2022

We spent about a week developing our ideas for the project. After having a preliminary plan, we developed the website and made a report according to the vision we had. We had many difficulties in building a website, learning about software to support game development, lack of people in development as well as report writing.

Finishing the project
May 8, 2022 - May 16, 2022

After having the ideas, we decided to develop the website first, and after 3 days, we moved on to authoring the report. Next, we used the rest of the time to finish our project, although it was challenging for us, we are happy that we have accomplished something we never had the experience to find out before.
